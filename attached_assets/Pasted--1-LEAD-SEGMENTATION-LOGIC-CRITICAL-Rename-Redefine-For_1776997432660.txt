## 1. 🔁 LEAD SEGMENTATION LOGIC (CRITICAL)

### Rename & Redefine: “Forgotten Leads”

Update logic and labeling:

**New Name:** `Re-Engagement Opportunities`

**Definition:**
Flag any lead that meets ALL of the following:

* Not contacted in **≥ 90 days**
* AND at least one of the following signals exists:

  * Wealth indicators (high income, assets, investor profile)
  * Job change / promotion
  * Inheritance indicators
  * Digital activity (email opens, clicks, site visits, WhatsApp replies)
  * Previously requested follow-up but **no action taken**
  * Marked for follow-up but **no logged activity**

**UI Changes:**

* Replace current color scheme with **high-contrast premium gradient (soft gold / lavender / pastel highlight)**
* Add **priority score badge**
* Add tooltip: *“Missed opportunity based on inactivity + high-value signals”*
